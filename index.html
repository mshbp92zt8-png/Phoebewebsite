Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Occupational therapy focuses on helping you participate in the activities that you want to do, you like to do, or need to do. For mental wellbeing, this means working on identifying your value, goals, your roles and environment that shape how you feel — and finding practical ways to make them more sustainable and meaningful.

Sessions are typically 50 minutes and take place via a secure video platform. We start with a free 15-minute introductory call so you can get a feel for the approach before committing.

Yes. I offer fully bilingual sessions in both English and Mandarin (Traditional Chinese). You can switch between languages mid-session — whatever feels most natural to you.

I offer OT coaching consultations — a structured, goal-focused approach grounded in occupational therapy principles. It is not psychotherapy or counselling, but it draws on evidence-based techniques to support your mental wellbeing and daily functioning.

I primarily work with adults based in the UK — students, working professionals, and parents — many of whom are navigating the unique challenges of living in the UK or abroad. I work with both Mandarin and English-speaking clients.
