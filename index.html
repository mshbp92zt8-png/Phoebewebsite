About

Hi, I'm Phoebe —
your OT for a more
fulfilling daily life.

I am an HCPC-registered occupational therapist specialising in mental health and wellbeing. Through online one-to-one consultations, I support adults in navigating life's challenges — whether that's burnout, adjustment to living abroad, managing stress, or finding a sustainable rhythm in your everyday.

I work with both English-speaking and Mandarin-speaking clients, offering a bilingual space where you can express yourself fully.

01

Your Voice Comes First

I deeply value what matters to you. Sessions are led by your priorities — there is no agenda imposed from the outside. You decide what we focus on and the pace at which we move.

02

Goals as a Compass

Together, we look into what you want from your life and find realistic, meaningful ways toward those goals. Progress doesn't have to be dramatic — small shifts often make the biggest difference.

03

A More Satisfying Daily Life

The aim of occupational therapy is practical and grounded: helping you participate in the activities that give your day meaning. Better routines, clearer values, more sustainable living — that is the work.
